#### Linear Dependence ####
If we have a se tof vectors $$S = \{v_{1}, v_{2}, ..., v_{n}}$$, they are **linearly dependent** iff I can 
find a $$c_{1}v_{1} + c_{2}v_{2} + .. + c_{n}v_{n} = \mathbf{0}$$, where at least one $$c_{i}$$ is non-zero.
